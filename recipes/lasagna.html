Lasagna

Description

Indulge in the savory layers of a classic lasagna with this delectable recipe that promises a symphony of flavors and textures. Begin by crafting a rich and aromatic tomato sauce, simmered to perfection with garlic, onions, and a medley of Italian herbs. Meanwhile, prepare a luscious filling, combining a velvety blend of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, along with finely chopped spinach and ground beef or sausage for a hearty touch. Assembling the lasagna becomes a delightful choreography as you layer al dente sheets of pasta with the sumptuous sauce and the creamy cheese mixture, creating a harmonious marriage of tastes.

The dish is then baked to golden perfection, allowing the cheeses to melt and mingle, while the pasta transforms into a tender, comforting embrace. With each forkful, experience the comforting warmth of this timeless Italian classic, where every bite tells a tale of culinary craftsmanship and familial indulgence.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 9-12 sheets, depending on the size of you baking dish)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef, pork or a combination
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces/800g) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (14 ounces/400g) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup (240ml) red wine (optional)
  • 15 ounces(425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 3 cups (about 340g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (about 100g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups (about 180g) chopped fresh spinach or frozen spinach, thawed and drained (optional)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 190C
  2. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Add chopped onions and minced garlic, saut√©ing until the onions are translucent.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. If desired, pour in red wine for added flavor. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese with a beaten egg, mixing until well blended. Add chopped spinach and fresh parsley if using, and mix thoroughly.
  5. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Assemble the lasagna in a baking dish. Begin with a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of lasagna noodles. Spread a portion of the ricotta mixture over the noodles, and sprinkle with mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Repeat the layers until the dish is filled, finishing with a layer of cheese on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes. Then, uncover and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Allow the lasagna to rest for about 10 minutes before slicing. This helps set the layers and makes it easier to serve.
  9. Serve the lasagna slices on plates, and enjoy this delicious, comforting dish!
